Our Story

Born from a Love of Tech

Exclusive Gadgets was founded in 2019 by a group of passionate tech enthusiasts who were frustrated by the lack of genuine, competitively priced premium technology in South Africa. We set out to change that.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across all nine provinces, from students buying their first laptop to professionals upgrading their home office, and from gamers seeking the latest console to families building smart home ecosystems.

Our mission is simple: bring the world's best technology to every South African, at honest prices, with exceptional service.
